Meet Our Founder

Leadership, vision, and commitment guide our strategic business solutions.

Francesco (Frank) Impastato

Founder & Managing Partner, Fiotto Consulting

A History of Financial Transformation

Frank Impastato is a distinguished figure in the IT business and Corporate Performance Management (CPM) sectors, with a career spanning several pivotal decades in digital finance. His journey culminated in the founding of Fiotto Consulting in 2024, a New York-based firm dedicated to guiding enterprises through seamless OneStream implementation projects.

Early Innovator in Digital Finance

Frank began his path with IMRS (International Managing Reporting Services), a pioneering company that helped launch digital finance alongside the advent of the personal computer. Here, he contributed to the development of early data consolidation and reporting software, such as FinalForm. Following a company rebrand, he transitioned to Hyperion Solutions Corporation, where he served for seven years as a financial consultant and project manager.

Deep OneStream Expertise

His deep specialization in CPM and OneStream began during his decade-long tenure as a Managing Partner at CompIntelligence, a firm officially recognized as an Authorized OneStream Training Provider. Furthermore, Frank utilized his expertise to manage the 11-year implementation and system update cycle for Brookfield Corporation, a major OneStream client.

The Fiotto Consulting Mission

The mission of Fiotto Consulting is rooted in Frank’s direct, hands-on experience with his clients. The firm stands out for its profound knowledge of OneStream, ensuring that clients achieve optimal results and a fluid execution of their financial performance transformation.
